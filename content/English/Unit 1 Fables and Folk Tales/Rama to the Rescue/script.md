# Story Script: Rama to the Rescue

## Scene 1: The Night Noise
**Visual:** Interior of a village house at night. Dark and quiet. A Man and his Wife are in bed, trying to sleep.
**Narration:** It was a quiet night in the village. Everyone was asleep, except for one couple.
**Dialogue:**
*   **Man:** (Whispering) What's the matter? What are you looking at?
*   **Wife:** Ssssh! Listen!
*   **Man:** Somebody is trying to get in.
*   **Wife:** I... I think he has got in... the noise has stopped.

## Scene 2: The Intruder
**Visual:** Outside the house, a Thief is quietly digging a hole through the mud wall. He pauses, listening.
**Narration:** A thief had dug his way to the house wall. He was waiting for the couple to fall asleep so he could steal their valuables.

## Scene 3: The Clever Plan
**Visual:** Inside. The Man leans close to his Wife and whispers a plan. She nods, understanding.
**Narration:** The man realized they were in danger. He came up with a clever plan. He whispered to his wife, "When he's in the house, I'll call out softly to him. Then I'll call out very loudly."

## Scene 4: The Trap
**Visual:** The Thief enters the room. Suddenly, the Man sits up.
**Dialogue:**
*   **Man:** (Softly) Rama?
*   **Thief:** (Freezes, thinking someone saw him)
*   **Man:** (Louder) Rama!
*   **Man:** (Yelling) RAMA! RAMA!

## Scene 5: The Rescue
**Visual:** Outside, Rama the Village Kotwal (policeman) is on patrol. He hears his name being screamed. He runs towards the house.
**Narration:** Rama, the village Kotwal, was patrolling nearby. He heard his name being called desperately. He ran towards the house.

## Scene 6: Caught Red-Handed
**Visual:** Rama the Kotwal bursts into the house (or catches the thief trying to escape through the hole). The Thief is surprised and terrified.
**Dialogue:**
*   **Rama:** Oh, Oh! Some thief has dug his way into this house!
*   **Thief:** (Trembling) Please, let me go!

## Scene 7: Safe and Sound
**Visual:** The villagers gather. The Thief is tied up. The Man and Wife thank Rama.
**Narration:** The villagers woke up and helped catch the thief. The couple thanked Rama for his quick response.
**Dialogue:**
*   **Wife:** Thank you, Rama!
*   **Rama:** Your clever trick saved you. You called the right name at the right time!
