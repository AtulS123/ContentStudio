# Story Script: The Chair

## Scene 1: The Boastful Boy
**Visual:** School playground. Mario is boasting to a group of kids about how many friends he has.
**Narration:** Mario loved to show off. He always bragged about how many friends he had and how popular he was at school.

## Scene 2: The Grandfather's Bet
**Visual:** Mario's home. Grandfather challenges Mario.
**Narration:** One day, his grandfather said, "Mario, I bet you don't have as many real friends as you think. Most are just companions." Mario accepted the bet.

## Scene 3: The Magic Chair
**Visual:** Grandfather brings an "invisible" chair (he's holding nothing). He mimes placing it down.
**Narration:** Grandpa brought out a "special" invisible chair. "If you manage to sit on this magic chair," he said, "you'll be able to tell who your real friends are."

## Scene 4: The Test
**Visual:** School break time. Mario gathers everyone. He tries to sit on "nothing" and falls hard. Kids laugh.
**Narration:** At school, Mario gathered everyone. He tried to sit on the invisible chair but fell flat on the ground. His classmates laughed at him.

## Scene 5: Persistence
**Visual:** Mario trying again and again, falling each time. The laughter grows.
**Narration:** Mario didn't give up. He kept trying and falling. Most kids just watched and laughed, enjoying the show.

## Scene 6: True Friends
**Visual:** Mario tries again, but this time Guneet, Asma, and Deepa rush forward and hold him up so he hovers in mid-air.
**Narration:** Suddenly, he didn't fall! He looked around and saw Guneet, Asma, and Deepa holding him up. They were his true friends who didn't want to see him hurt.

## Scene 7: The Realization
**Visual:** Mario walking away with his three friends, leaving the laughing crowd behind.
**Narration:** Mario realized the magic. True friends are those who care for you, not those who laugh at your bad luck. He had lost the bet but found his real friends.
