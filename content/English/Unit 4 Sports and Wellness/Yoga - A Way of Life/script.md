# Story Script: Yoga - A Way of Life

## Scene 1: Origins
**Visual:** Ancient India. Sage Patanjali writing the 'Yoga Sutras'. Background of nature and temples.
**Narration:** Yoga is a practice from ancient India. The word comes from 'Yuj', meaning to unite. Sage Patanjali described it as a way to quiet the mind.

## Scene 2: Physical Benefits
**Visual:** People doing various Asanas (postures). Stretching, balancing.
**Narration:** Yoga helps stretch and strengthen muscles. It improves flexibility, balance, and reduces body pain.

## Scene 3: Mental Peace
**Visual:** A person meditating in a calm environment. Breathing deeply (Pranayama).
**Narration:** It involves breathing techniques and meditation. This calms the mind, reduces stress, and improves concentration.

## Scene 4: Health Boost
**Visual:** Diagram of a human body showing heart, blood circulation, and immune system glowing.
**Narration:** Yoga boosts the immune system, improves heart health, and helps in weight loss. It promotes overall well-being.

## Scene 5: Global Reach
**Visual:** People of different nationalities practicing yoga. The WHO mYoga App logo.
**Narration:** Today, yoga is practiced worldwide. The WHO even launched an app to help people practice it anywhere.

## Scene 6: The Practice
**Visual:** A diverse group of people practicing yoga together in a park, moving in sync with their breath.
**Narration:** Remember, yoga is not just exercise. It is the union of body and mind. When practiced with awareness, it becomes a way of life.
